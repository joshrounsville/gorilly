  • About Gorilly

  • What is Gorilly?

    Gorilly is a showrooming web application that enables shoppers to try products in person before buying them online directly from the manufacturer. It's a way to try before you buy to make sure you're getting what you want.

  • What is showrooming?

    Showrooming is the practice of seeing products in person before buying them direct online from the product manufacturers. Gorilly makes it easy and safe to showroom to ensure you love what you buy.

  • How does Gorilly work?

    By connecting the shopper and Product Gorilly, shoppers are able to see products before buying direct online, and Product Gorillys make some extra cash. For more information, please visit How It Works.

  • What types of products are on Gorilly?

    Gorilly believes in great products that are well-made and provide true value to the customer. Gorilly is currently working with three brands for its beta launch in San Francisco. Stay tuned for new brands. If you would like to recommend a new brand or manufacturer, please let us know.

  • Do I get to borrow products?

    No. Shoppers meet with Product Gorillys to experience products in person. The shopper can see, feel and experience the product and ask questions — in–person experience and real feedback make it the best judge of product quality. The shopper and Product Gorilly then go their separate ways and the shopper can purchase the product directly from the manufacturer's website using a special code provided by the Product Gorilly.

  • Where and how do I buy products?

    Products are purchased directly through each brand's own e-commerce site. Go to the manufacturer's site and proceed through the normal ordering process, using the promotion code provided by the Product Gorilly during checkout.

  • Do I get a discount for using Gorilly?

    There is always an incentive for using the special code provided by a Product Gorilly. The discounts and incentives vary by manufacturer. Discounts may be up to 10% and incentives may include a free additional item with a purchase.

  • How do I get a promotion code for a product?

    In order to get a promotion code for a product you must contact a Product Gorilly. Product Gorillys will then provide a unique promotion code for a specific brand to be used on the brand's own e-commerce site. To get started, search for a product near you.

  • Will my promotion code expire?

    Yes, however the life of each promotion code depends on the brand. We will always let you know when your promotion code is about to expire. Also, if there is any misuse of a promotion code it will be cancelled.

  • What if I don't see a product or brand I'm interested in?

    Gorilly believes in great products that are well-made and provide true value to the customer. Gorilly is currently working with three brands for its beta launch in San Francisco. Stay tuned for new brands. If you would like to recommend a new brand or manufacturer, please let us know.

  • For Product Gorillys

  • What is a Product Gorilly?

    A product Gorilly is someone who owns a great product and wants to show their products to others because...

    1. they believe in the importance of trying before you buy
    2. they enjoy earning extra cash from Gorilly Kickback

  • How much do I get paid?

    Product Gorillys get paid a kickback (commission) on any sale made using any of the special codes they provide to shoppers. Kickbacks are 10% of the price paid online by the shopper after any discounts. For example, if the product normally sells for $500 and there is a 5% discount for the shopper, the shopper would pay $475 ($500 â€“ 5%), and the Product Gorilly would earn $47.50 (10% of $475).

  • How do I get paid?

    When a sale is made, Gorilly will pay Product Gorillys directly through their preferred payment method. There are three kickback (payment) methods: PayPal, Venmo, and mailed check. Here is a summary of payment fees and details by method (for additional information regarding payment, please go to PayPal or Venmo, or contact us):

    Kickback Method: Fees *Timing of Kickback Currency
    PayPal 2.9% + $0.30 Instant USD
    Venmo Free Instant USD
    Mailed Check Free Once a month USD

    *Payment is made once your order has cleared with the manufacturer.

  • When do I get paid?

    Gorilly does its best to pay Product Gorillys as soon as humanly possible. We want you to be rewarded. Payment usually happens within 24 hours of a product's sale (we need to wait for credit card transactions to clear on manufacturers' websites). When a sale comes through we'll pay you using your preferred payment method.

  • How do I change my payment method?

    You can view and change your payment method by going to Profile & Settings.

  • Will my promotion codes expire?

    Yes, however the life of each promotion code depends on the brand. We will always let you know when your promotion code is about to expire. Also, if there is any misuse of a promotion code it will be cancelled.

  • Where can I see My Listings and Codes?

    Under My Listings and Codes in your account.

  • How do I edit a listing?

    Go to My Listings and Codes, select the listing you would like to edit, and make any updates or changes before saving.

  • Can I temporarily deactivate a listing? If so, how do I do it?

    Yes. Go to My Listings and Codes and change the status of the listing from On to Off. To reverse this and reactivate your listing, change back to On.
