Gorilly Privacy Statement

  • What information does Gorilly Inc. collect?

    Gorilly Inc. collects information from you when you utilize the Gorilly website, subscribe to the Gorilly newsletter or fill out a form. When registering on the Gorilly website, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name and/or email address. You may, however, visit the Gorilly website anonymously.

  • What does Gorilly Inc. use your information for?

    Any of the information collected from you may be used in one of the following ways:

    • To improve the Gorilly website (Gorilly Inc. continually strives to improve the Gorilly website offerings based on the information and feedback received from you)
    • To improve customer service (your information helps Gorilly Inc. to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs)
    • To send periodic emails (note: If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, please follow the detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email.)

  • Does the Gorilly website use cookies?

    The Gorilly website does not use cookies.

  • Does Gorilly Inc. sell any information to outside parties?

    Gorilly Inc. does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist in operating the Gorilly website, conducting Gorilly Inc. business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. Gorilly Inc. may also release your information when Gorilly Inc. believes its release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce the Gorilly site policies, or protect Gorilly Inc.’s or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

  • California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

    Because Gorilly Inc. values your privacy Gorilly Inc. has taken the necessary precautions to be in compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. Gorilly Inc. therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.

  • Online Privacy Policy Only

    This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through the Gorilly website and not to information collected offline.

  • Your Consent

    By using the Gorilly site, you consent to our website’s privacy policy.

  • Changes to our Privacy Policy

    If Gorilly Inc. decides to change its privacy policy, Gorilly Inc. will post those changes on this page. This policy was last modified on September 3, 2014.

  • Contacting Us

    If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy you may contact Gorilly Inc. using the information below.

    www.gorilly.com

    info@gorilly.com
