About Gorilly

Your Showroom

Gorilly was started by two lifelong friends who share a passion for great products. Advancements in online shopping have made buying great products from virtually anywhere easy, however the opportunity to try products in person before buying them has not kept pace. They believe this is the ultimate test for making smart purchases.

The pair started by converting a an old step truck into a high-end showroom space. They hit the mean streets of the Bay Area and allowed people to try products in person before buying online.

But the truck was just too darn small – too many people weren’t afforded the opportunity to showroom great products. So they built Gorilly, a showrooming network that allows people to try great products before buying them online, and the coolest thing is... it’s as simple as two people meeting (I guess you can call it a true “social network”).
