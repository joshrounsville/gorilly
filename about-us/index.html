About Gorilly

Your Showroom

Gorilly was started by two lifelong friends who share a passion for great products. Advancements in online shopping have made buying great products from virtually anywhere easy, however the opportunity to try products in person before buying them has not kept pace. At Gorilly we believe tactile experience is the ultimate test for making smart purchases and the best way to further grow (or to help) great brands that do not broadly provide customers with opportunities to experience in-person.

The Gorilly saga has been quite the adventure — the two (with the help of a friend) started by converting an old step-truck into a high-end showroom space. They hit the mean streets of the Bay Area, allowing people to try products in person before buying online. The truck became much more than a vehicle — it became a new way to shop for beautifully-designed and well-made products that customers normally couldn't get their hands on before buying. The movement struck a chord with the people of San Francisco.

But the truck was just too darn small — too many people weren't afforded the opportunity to showroom great products. So they built Gorilly, a showrooming network that allows people to try great products before buying them online, and the coolest thing is... it's as simple as two people meeting.
